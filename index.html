How to play

Control your snake to eat food and grow long without hitting the walls. Last longer and eat more food to get a higher score!
Desktop Controls: Arrow keys to move, spacebar to pause and play.
Mobile Controls: Press either of the two top buttons to go up, either of the two bottom buttons to go down. Similarly, press either of the two left buttons to go left and either of the two right buttons to go right. Tap the game screen to pause and play.
